Software based Simulation

Software based simulation plays a major role in the aerospace industry like finalising the configuration, design missions, validation of NGC (Navigation, Guidance and Control) algorithms, validation of hardware compatibility, range safety, etc. Simulation provides the cost effective and time efficient integrated testing of hardware and algorithms. In addition, simulations provide a platform to understand individual subsystems and its interfaces.
